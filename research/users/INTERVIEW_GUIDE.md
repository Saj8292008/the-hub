## Core Questions (All Personas)

### Opening (5 min)
1. Tell me about yourself and how you got into [flipping/collecting/deal hunting].
2. How long have you been doing this?
3. On a scale of 1-10, how satisfied are you with your current tools/process?

### Current State (10 min)
4. Walk me through your typical [day/week] when it comes to finding deals.
5. What tools or apps do you currently use?
6. What's working well?
7. What's frustrating or broken?

### Pain Points (10 min)
8. Tell me about a time you missed a deal you really wanted.
9. What's the most time-consuming part of your process?
10. What's something you wish existed that doesn't?

### Solution Exploration (10 min)
11. If you had a magic wand, what would the perfect tool do?
12. How much would you pay for something that [solves their #1 pain]?
13. What would make you switch from your current setup?

### Closing (5 min)
14. What would make you recommend a tool to a friend?
15. Anything else you want to share?
16. Can we follow up if we have more questions?

## Persona-Specific Deep Dives

### For Resellers (Marcus)

**Inventory:**
- How many items do you typically have listed at once?
- How do you currently track your inventory?
- Show me your spreadsheet/system (if they're willing)
- What happens when you can't find something?

**Profit:**
- How do you calculate if a deal is worth it?
- Do you track your true profit after fees?
- What's the most you've lost because of poor tracking?
- How do platform fee changes affect you?

**Sourcing:**
- Where do you find most of your inventory?
- How do you know if a price is good?
- Do you use any apps while sourcing in stores?

**Scale:**
- What's preventing you from scaling up?
- How many hours/week do you spend on admin vs. selling?

---

### For Collectors (Jamie)

**The Hunt:**
- What's your current "grail" you're searching for?
- How long have you been looking?
- How do you currently track prices?

**Alerts:**
- What alert tools have you tried?
- Tell me about alerts that worked (or failed).
- How fast do you need to know about a restock?

**Trust:**
- How do you verify authenticity?
- Have you ever been scammed?
- What would make you trust a platform/seller?

**Community:**
- Are you part of any collector communities?
- How do you share finds with others?

---

### For Bargain Hunters (Sarah)

**Discovery:**
- How do you currently find deals?
- How many deal sites do you check regularly?
- What categories matter most to you?

**Decision Making:**
- How do you decide if something is "a good deal"?
- Do you research prices before buying?
- Do you ever regret not waiting for a better price?

**Notifications:**
- How do you prefer to be notified about deals?
- How many notifications is too many?
- Email vs. push vs. in-app?

**Simple vs. Powerful:**
- Would you rather have simple or detailed price info?
- Do charts help you or overwhelm you?

---

### For Dealers (Tony)

**Operations:**
- How many SKUs do you manage?
- What's your current inventory system?
- How many people on your team?

**Sourcing:**
- How do you source at scale?
- Do you buy liquidation/wholesale?
- What data do you need before buying?

**Integration:**
- What other software does this need to work with?
- Do you need accounting integration?
- API access important?

**Support:**
- What level of support do you expect?
- Would you pay more for faster support?
- What's your tolerance for downtime?

## Competitive Intelligence Questions

Don't ask directly about competitors. Instead:

- "What made you choose [tool they mentioned]?"
- "What would make you stop using [tool]?"
- "If [tool] shut down tomorrow, what would you do?"
- "What does [tool] do well that others don't?"
