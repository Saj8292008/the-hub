## StockX: Best for Liquidity and Speed

**Overview:** Anonymous marketplace with bid/ask system. Largest user base (10M+ monthly).

### Pricing
- **Buyer fee:** 3% transaction fee + $13.95-25 shipping
- **Seller fee:** 9.5% (first-time), 9% (basic), 8.5% (level 3+)
- **Payment processing:** 3%

**Example:**
- Jordan 1 Chicago listed at $2,000
- Buyer pays: $2,060 + $20 shipping = **$2,080 total**
- Seller receives: $2,000 - $190 (9.5%) = **$1,810**
- **Platform takes:** $270 (13%)

### Authentication Process
- **Location:** Detroit, Hong Kong, London facilities
- **Turnaround:** 1-3 business days
- **Pass rate:** ~98% (2% rejected as fake/damaged)
- **Quality:** Good, but occasional fakes slip through
- **Guarantee:** Full refund if fake detected post-purchase

### Pros
✅ **Fastest transactions** - Instant buy available
✅ **Most liquid market** - Easy to buy/sell quickly
✅ **Transparent pricing** - Real-time bid/ask spreads
✅ **Portfolio tracking** - Track your collection value

### Cons
❌ **Deadstock only** - No used sneakers
❌ **Authentication misses** - ~1% fake pass rate
❌ **High fees** - 13% total fees (both sides)
❌ **Customer service** - Slow response times

### Best Use Cases
- You need to buy/sell quickly
- You're trading hype releases (Jordan 1, Dunk, Yeezy)
- You want transparent market pricing
- You're buying deadstock only

## Peer-to-Peer: Facebook, Instagram, Reddit

**Overview:** Direct buyer-to-seller transactions. No fees, no authentication, high scam risk.

### Pricing
- **Buyer fee:** $0
- **Seller fee:** $0
- **Payment:** PayPal Goods & Services (3.49% + $0.49) recommended

### Pros
✅ **No platform fees** - Lowest prices (20-30% below StockX)
✅ **Direct negotiation** - Haggle freely
✅ **Local meetups** - Inspect before buying

### Cons
❌ **No authentication** - Must verify yourself
❌ **High scam risk** - 40%+ of listings are fakes
❌ **No buyer protection** - Venmo/Zelle = no recourse
❌ **Time-consuming** - Vetting sellers takes effort

### How to Avoid Scams
1. **Only use PayPal Goods & Services** (chargeback protection)
2. **Request tagged photos** (username + date written on paper)
3. **Check seller history** (reddit karma, FB profile age, references)
4. **Meet in public** for local deals (police station parking lots)
5. **Use legit check services** ($3-30 for authentication)
6. **Trust your gut** - If deal seems too good, it's fake

### Best Use Cases
- You're experienced authenticating sneakers
- You're buying from trusted community members
- You want absolute lowest prices
- You're willing to take on risk

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Which sneaker resale platform has the lowest fees?**
A: For buyers: eBay ($0 buyer fees). For sellers: Facebook/Instagram (0% but risky). Among authenticated platforms, GOAT and StockX tie at ~9.5% seller fees.

**Q: Is StockX or GOAT better?**
A: StockX for speed and liquidity (instant buy/sell). GOAT for authentication quality and used sneakers. StockX if trading hype releases, GOAT if buying expensive grails.

**Q: Does eBay authenticate sneakers?**
A: Yes, free authentication on all sneakers $100+. Handled by SneakerCon. Quality is good (~97% accuracy) but slower than StockX/GOAT (adds 3-5 days shipping).

**Q: Can you get scammed on StockX or GOAT?**
A: Very rare but possible. StockX ~1% fake pass rate. GOAT ~0.5%. Both offer full refunds if fake detected post-purchase. Use platform messaging only, never go off-platform.

**Q: What's the best platform for selling used sneakers?**
A: GOAT (only major platform accepting used 8/10+). Can sell for 30-50% of deadstock value. Alternative: Facebook groups if experienced.

**Q: Should I buy sneakers on eBay or StockX?**
A: eBay for deals (10-15% cheaper, free auth). StockX for speed and popular releases (better inventory). eBay best for patient buyers, StockX for instant gratification.

**Q: How do I avoid fake sneakers?**
A: Use authenticated platforms (StockX, GOAT, eBay $100+). If buying peer-to-peer, use legit check services (CheckCheck $3, Legitmark $10-30), only PayPal G&S, request tagged photos.

**Q: Which platform ships fastest?**
A: Stadium Goods/Flight Club (instant ship, pre-authenticated). StockX next (3-7 days). GOAT slowest (consignment model, 7-14 days). eBay varies (5-10 days with auth).
