**Before sending:**
1. Personalize the [BRACKETS] - generic emails get ignored
2. Research their content - mention something specific

4. Follow up once (not more) after 5-7 days

- Keep under 50 characters
- Avoid spam triggers (FREE, $$, ACT NOW)

## ‚ùå What NOT to Do

1. **Don't spam** ‚Äì One email + one follow-up max
2. **Don't be generic** ‚Äì "[BLOG]" is obvious template language
3. **Don't oversell** ‚Äì Let the product speak for itself
4. **Don't lie** ‚Äì About metrics, commissions, or anything
5. **Don't mass BCC** ‚Äì Individual emails only
6. **Don't attach files** ‚Äì Links only (attachments = spam filters)
