# Instagram DM Outreach Template

## Pre-Outreach (Important!)

Before DMing:
1. **Follow them** (at least 24 hours before)
2. **Like 3-5 posts** (spread out over a few days)
3. **Leave 1-2 genuine comments** (not generic "nice!")
4. **Watch their stories** and react occasionally

This warms them up and shows you're not spam.

## Timing Tips

**Best times to DM:**
- Weekdays: 8-10am or 7-9pm (their timezone)
- Avoid weekends (lower response rates)
- Don't DM right after they post (looks automated)

**Response expectations:**
- Small accounts (< 10k): 30-50% response rate
- Medium (10-100k): 15-25% response rate
- Large (100k+): 5-10% response rate

---

## Red Flags (Don't Waste Time)

- "DM for paid promos only"
- Never respond after 2 follow-ups
- Only post sponsored content (no authentic voice)
- Engagement looks fake (comments all generic)
