## Pro Tips

1. **Personalize** - Mention a recent deal they posted that was good
2. **Keep it short** - Telegram culture is brief
3. **Offer first** - Say you'll post about them first to build trust
4. **Time it right** - Post during peak hours (10am-2pm, 6pm-9pm)
