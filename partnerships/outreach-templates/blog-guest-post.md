# Blog Guest Post Pitch Template

## Research First

Before pitching:
1. Read 5+ recent articles on the blog
2. Check if they have contributor guidelines
3. Note their writing style and topics covered
4. See who else has guest posted

---

## Initial Pitch Email

**Subject:** Guest post pitch: "How to Never Overpay for a [Watch/Sneaker] Again"

---

Hey [EDITOR NAME],

I've been reading [BLOG NAME] for a while - really enjoyed [SPECIFIC RECENT ARTICLE] on [TOPIC].

I'm the founder of The Hub, a price tracking tool for watches and sneakers. We analyze thousands of listings to find underpriced deals.

**I'd love to write a guest post for your readers:**

**Title options:**
1. "7 Data-Backed Strategies to Find Watch Deals in 2026"
2. "The Price Drop Calendar: When to Buy Your Next Watch"
3. "How I Built a Tool That Finds Underpriced Watches (And What the Data Taught Me)"

**What I'll include:**
- Real data from our price tracking (not fluff)
- Actionable tips readers can use today
- Examples with specific models and price points

**About me:**
- Built The Hub (thehub.deals)
- Track 50,000+ listings monthly
- Previously wrote for [any relevant credits]

Would any of these work for [BLOG NAME]?

[Your name]

## Article Structure That Works

**Hook** (100 words)
- Start with a compelling stat or story
- "Last month, someone bought a $4,000 watch for $2,100..."

**The Problem** (200 words)
- Why it's hard to find deals
- Information asymmetry

**The Solution/Framework** (800-1,000 words)
- Your main tips/strategies
- Use data to back up claims
- Include specific examples

**How-To Section** (300 words)
- Practical steps readers can take
- Mention your tool naturally here

**Conclusion** (100 words)
- Summary
- Soft CTA

---

## Link Placement Best Practices

**DO:**
- Link where it adds genuine value
- Use descriptive anchor text ("price tracking tools")
- Limit to 1-2 links max
- Include in author bio

**DON'T:**
- Force links where they don't fit
- Use exact-match anchor text
- Stuff multiple links
- Sound promotional

## After Publication

1. **Thank the editor** - Brief email
2. **Share on your channels** - Social, newsletter, Telegram
3. **Respond to comments** - Engage with readers
4. **Track referral traffic** - Note in partnership tracker
5. **Pitch again in 2-3 months** - Build the relationship
