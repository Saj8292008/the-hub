## ğŸ¨ Design Patterns Used

### Colors
- **Primary Gradient:** `from-blue-500 to-purple-600`
- **Background:** `bg-gray-950` / `bg-gray-900`
- **Text:** `text-white` / `text-gray-400`
- **Accents:** Green (success), Red (error), Yellow (featured)

### Animations
- **Entrance:** `fadeInUp` on scroll trigger
- **Hover:** `y: -8px` lift + glow
- **Floating:** Continuous y-axis motion
- **Counters:** Animated count-up
- **Accordion:** Height transition

### Typography
- **Headings:** `text-4xl` to `text-7xl`, `font-bold`
- **Body:** `text-base` to `text-xl`
- **Gradient Text:** `bg-clip-text text-transparent`

### Spacing
- **Section Padding:** `py-24` (96px vertical)
- **Container:** `max-w-7xl mx-auto`
- **Component Padding:** `p-6` to `p-8`
- **Gaps:** `gap-8` to `gap-12`
