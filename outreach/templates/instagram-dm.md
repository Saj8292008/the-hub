## Instagram DM Best Practices

1. **Engage first** - Like 2-3 posts, maybe comment, before DMing
2. **Reply to stories** - Highest open rates, feels natural
3. **No links first message** - Builds trust, avoids spam filters
4. **Use emojis but don't overdo** - Match their energy
5. **Be brief** - They're scrolling, not reading essays
6. **Follow up once** - If no response after 3-5 days, one polite bump
7. **Time it right** - Evening/night when people are browsing
8. **Don't pitch in comments** - Always take to DMs
