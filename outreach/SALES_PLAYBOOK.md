## Target Customer Profile

### Primary Segments

| Segment | Description | Pain Points | Value Prop |

| **Power Resellers** | 10+ sales/month, multi-platform | Time copying listings, inventory tracking, double-sells | Automation, sync, efficiency |

| **Small Dealers** | Semi-pro, Instagram + local | No real system, losing track | Professional tools at indie price |

## Outreach Strategy

### The 3-Touch Approach

**Touch 1: Warm Up (Optional but powerful)**
- Engage with their content first
- Like posts, reply to story, comment genuinely
- Creates familiarity before the pitch

**Touch 2: Initial Outreach**
- Send personalized DM referencing specific content
- Focus on THEIR pain, not your features
- Ask for feedback, not a sale
- No links in first message

**Touch 3: Follow Up**
- If no response after 4-5 days, one polite bump
- Add new value (article, tip, feature they'd like)
- If still nothing, move on

### Channel-Specific Tactics

| Platform | Approach | Timing | Key Tip |
|----------|----------|--------|---------|
| Reddit DM | Reference specific listing | Afternoon | Check they have DMs open |
| Instagram | Reply to story first | Evening | Use voice DM to stand out |
| Discord | Be active in server first | After chatting publicly | Don't mass DM |
| Email | Very personalized | Tue-Thu morning | 5 sentences max |

## Message Framework

### The SPIN Formula

1. **Situation**: Reference their current state
   - "Saw you sell on Reddit and eBay..."

2. **Problem**: Name a pain they likely have
   - "Managing listings across platforms is a pain..."

3. **Implication**: What it costs them
   - "Probably losing time or had a double-sell..."

4. **Need**: Your solution
   - "Built The Hub to solve exactly this..."

### Key Phrases That Work

**Opening:**
- "Quick question about your [watch/sneaker] business..."
- "Saw your [SPECIFIC ITEM] post - gorgeous piece"
- "Noticed you're one of the top sellers on..."

**Problem:**
- "Do you ever get tired of..."
- "How do you currently handle..."
- "Curious if you've dealt with..."

**Ask:**
- "Would love your feedback on..."
- "Would you be interested in checking it out?"
- "Can I show you what we've built?"

**Close:**
- "No pressure either way"
- "Even if not interested, would love your thoughts"
- "Early access is free - just want feedback"

## Objection Handling

### "I'm good with spreadsheets"
"Totally get it - spreadsheets work! The Hub is basically spreadsheets on autopilot. You enter once, it syncs everywhere. Would it save you time if you didn't have to manually update 5 platforms when something sells?"

### "I don't have time"
"That's exactly why I built this - to save time. 5 minutes to try it could save you hours per week. Worth a quick look?"

### "I only sell on [one platform]"
"Makes sense to focus. If you ever expand, we'll be here. Mind if I check back in a few months?"

### "What does it cost?"
"For beta testers, it's free while we're building. Just looking for feedback from real sellers like you."

### "How do I know it's legit?"
"Fair question. Here's my [Reddit profile / sales history / etc]. Happy to hop on a call so you can ask questions."

### "I've tried other tools"
"What didn't work for you? Genuinely curious - helps me build something better. The Hub does [specific differentiator]."

## Tracking & Metrics

### Use tracker.json

Every contact should be logged with:
- Platform & segment
- Date of first/last contact
- Current status
- Notes on conversation
- Follow-up date if needed

### KPIs to Track

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Response Rate | 20-30% | If lower, refine messaging |
| Interest Rate | 10-15% | Of those who respond |
| Conversion Rate | 5-10% | Of those interested |
| Messages/Day | 5-10 | Quality > quantity |

### Weekly Review
- How many contacted?
- Response rate by platform?
- What messaging worked?
- Any patterns in rejections?
