## ðŸŽ¯ Reselling Niche Insights

**Community mindset:**
- Highly entrepreneurial, always looking for edge
- Platform fees are HUGE pain point
- Trust/scam avoidance is critical
- Success stories drive engagement
- Data and analytics are valued

**What they care about:**
- Lower fees = higher margins
- Speed of sales
- Buyer/seller protection
- Authentication (for sneakers, watches, designer)
- Analytics and pricing tools
- Community/knowledge sharing

**Competitive landscape:**
- eBay - Dominant, fees ~13%+
- Poshmark - Clothing focus, 20% fee
- Mercari - Lower fees, growing
- StockX/GOAT - Sneakers, high fees
- Grailed - Menswear/streetwear
- Facebook Marketplace - Free but risky
- OfferUp/Craigslist - Local, free

**Key value props for The Hub:**
- Lower fees than competitors
- Better analytics/price tracking
- Cross-category support (watches + sneakers + general)
- Community features
- Authentication options
