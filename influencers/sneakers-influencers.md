## ðŸŽ¯ Sneaker Niche Insights

**What works:**
- Hype awareness - know the releases, the collabs
- Value focus - deals, steals, price comparison
- Authenticity - legit check content is huge
- Speed - this market moves FAST

**Culture notes:**
- Sneakerheads are protective of the culture
- Don't be a "reseller villain" - controversial topic
- Know the difference between sneaker collectors and hypebeasts
- Regional scenes matter (NYC, LA, Chicago, etc.)

**Key value props for The Hub:**
- Price tracking across platforms
- Authentication features
- Fee comparison vs. StockX, GOAT, eBay
- Faster selling/buying process
- Community verification

**Competitive awareness:**
- StockX - market leader, fees are pain point
- GOAT - trusted authentication
- eBay - huge inventory, auth program
- Grailed - used/vintage market
- Tradeblock - trade platform
