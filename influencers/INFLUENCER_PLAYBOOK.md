## üéØ Outreach Strategy by Tier

### Micro Influencers (1K-10K)
**Why they matter:** High engagement rates (often 5-15%), authentic communities, eager to partner
**Approach:** 
- DM on their primary platform
- Offer free Hub access + feature on your platform
- Quick turnaround, low negotiation

### Mid Influencers (10K-100K)  
**Why they matter:** Established credibility, growing momentum, reasonable rates
**Approach:**
- Find business email (usually in bio/about)
- Propose affiliate partnership (10-20% commission)
- Offer exclusive early access or features
- 2-4 week response timeline

### Macro Influencers (100K+)
**Why they matter:** Instant credibility, massive reach, PR value
**Approach:**
- Contact through management/agency
- Be prepared to pay ($500-$5000+ per post)
- Lead with data: your user numbers, growth, unique value
- 4-8 week timeline, multiple touchpoints

## ‚ö†Ô∏è What NOT to Do

1. **Don't mass-spam** - Personalization matters
2. **Don't lie about metrics** - They'll find out
3. **Don't demand exclusivity** from small creators
4. **Don't lowball** established creators
5. **Don't ghost** after they create content
6. **Don't be weird** - They're people, not billboards

## üìà Tracking Success

For each partnership, track:
- **Reach:** Views, impressions
- **Engagement:** Likes, comments, shares
- **Conversions:** Signups, sales via their link/code
- **CAC:** Cost per acquisition (spend √∑ conversions)
- **LTV impact:** Do their referrals stick around?

**Good CAC benchmarks:**
- Micro: $1-5 per user
- Mid: $5-15 per user  
- Macro: $10-30 per user
