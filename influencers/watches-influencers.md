## ðŸŽ¯ Watch Niche Insights

**What works:**
- Authenticity - watch people hate shills
- Specific knowledge - know the references, movements, history
- Value proposition - how does The Hub help collectors?
- Trust - this community is wary of scams

**What to avoid:**
- Don't call a watch "cheap" - say "affordable"
- Don't confuse brands/models
- Don't push too hard - watch people are patient buyers
- Don't compare to grey market dealers negatively (many creators have relationships)

**Key value props for The Hub:**
- Price transparency/tracking
- Authentication/trust
- Community features
- Selling without massive fees
