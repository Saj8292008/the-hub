## Monthly Schedule

### Week 1: Community AMA

**Event:** Monthly AMA with Team
**When:** First Tuesday, 7 PM CT
**Duration:** 60 minutes
**Platform:** Discord voice + Telegram live

**Preparation (Week Before):**
- [ ] Announce AMA 7 days out
- [ ] Collect questions in advance
- [ ] Prepare talking points
- [ ] Set up tech (voice channel, recording)

**AMA Agenda:**
```
00-05: Welcome & housekeeping
05-15: Product updates & roadmap
15-30: Market insights discussion
30-55: Community Q&A
55-60: Wrap-up & next month preview
```

**Post-AMA:**
- [ ] Post summary/highlights
- [ ] Share recording (if recorded)
- [ ] Follow up on unanswered questions

---

### Week 2: Expert Guest

**Event:** Industry Expert AMA or Interview
**When:** Second Thursday, 7 PM CT
**Duration:** 45-60 minutes
**Platform:** Discord voice or recorded

**Guest Categories:**
- Watch dealers/collectors
- Car restoration experts
- Sneaker authentication specialists
- Market analysts
- Successful flippers/collectors

**Preparation:**
- [ ] Confirm guest 3 weeks out
- [ ] Collect community questions
- [ ] Prepare intro and talking points
- [ ] Promote across platforms

**Post-Event:**
- [ ] Thank you to guest (public + private)
- [ ] Share highlights/quotes
- [ ] Content repurpose (blog post, clips)

## Posting Frequency Guidelines

### By Platform

| Platform | Frequency | Type |
|----------|-----------|------|
| **Telegram Channel** | 3-5x/day | Deals + daily posts |
| **Telegram Group** | As needed | Responses + engagement |
| **Discord** | 5-7x/day | Mix of content + engagement |
| **Email Newsletter** | 1x/day | Digest at 8 AM |
| **Blog** | 2-4x/week | SEO + educational |
| **Reddit** | 2-3x/week | Value-add posts |
| **Twitter/X** | 3-5x/day | Deals + engagement |

### Content Mix

Aim for this balance weekly:
- 40% Deal content (alerts, highlights, recaps)
- 25% Engagement (polls, questions, discussions)
- 20% Educational (tutorials, guides, tips)
- 10% Community (spotlights, celebrations)
- 5% Promotional (Premium, features)
