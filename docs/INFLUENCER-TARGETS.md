# The Hub - Influencer Partnership Targets

*Micro-influencers (under 100k followers) in watch & sneaker space*

---

## Why Micro-Influencers?

- **Higher engagement rates** (5-8% vs 1-2% for large accounts)
- **More affordable** (often free product/access is enough)
- **Authentic communities** (followers actually trust them)
- **Hungry for partnerships** (more responsive to outreach)
- **Niche alignment** (their audience = our users)

## Outreach Strategy

### Phase 1: Build Relationship (Week 1-2)
1. Follow on all platforms
2. Engage genuinely with content (comments, likes, shares)
3. Share their content to our channels with credit
4. Reply to stories, participate in community

### Phase 2: Soft Intro (Week 3)
- DM: "Hey [name], been following your content on [specific topic]. Built something your audience might dig - a deal alert tool for watches/sneakers. Would love to give you free access to check out. No strings."

### Phase 3: Partnership Pitch (Week 4+)
If they respond positively, escalate to:
- Free premium access (lifetime)
- Custom affiliate link (commission on signups)
- Exclusive deals to share with their audience
- Co-branded content opportunities
