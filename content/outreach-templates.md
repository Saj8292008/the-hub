## Response Tracking

**When they respond positively**:
1. Send demo link + login immediately
2. Create custom referral code: `[CHANNELNAME]HUB` or `HUB[NAME]`
3. Schedule follow-up in 3-5 days to check if they tried it
4. Ask what they think + what features would help them most

**When they ask about money**:
- Be transparent: We're not paying for posts (startup budget)
- Offer: Revenue share on their referral conversions (if we monetize)
- Emphasize: Free tool for them + their audience, genuine value-add

**When they ghost**:
- One follow-up after 5-7 days
- If still nothing, move on
- No triple-texting (looks desperate)
