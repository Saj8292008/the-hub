## Risk Mitigation

**Affiliate Program Changes:**
- Diversify across multiple platforms (don't rely on one)
- Build direct relationships with brands/dealers
- Maintain strong user trust (never promote bad deals for commissions)

**Competition:**
- Focus on curation quality over quantity
- Build community features (comments, upvotes, user submissions)
- Personalization and AI-powered deal matching

**Platform Risk:**
- Own the email list (export regularly)
- Build on web first, apps second
- Don't depend solely on social media traffic
