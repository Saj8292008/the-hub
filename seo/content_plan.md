## SEO Optimization Checklist

For every piece of content:

### Before Publishing
- [ ] Target keyword in title
- [ ] Target keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Target keyword in at least one H2
- [ ] Meta description written (150-160 chars)
- [ ] URL slug optimized
- [ ] Images have alt text with keywords
- [ ] Internal links to related content (3-5)
- [ ] External links to authoritative sources (2-3)

### After Publishing
- [ ] Submit to Google Search Console
- [ ] Share on social media
- [ ] Share in Telegram community
- [ ] Post teaser on Reddit (if appropriate)
- [ ] Email to newsletter subscribers
